Yan Leng

Assistant professor in McCombs School of Business, the University of Texas at Austin. I am a core member in UT Machine Learning Lab and affliate with the UT AI and Misinformation Initiative.

I am a computational social scientist and network scientist using large-scale data sets, network theory, and machine learning techniques to understand human behavior over social networks.

Email: yan.leng@mccombs.utexas.edu

Twitter: Follow @lylengyan


I obtained my Ph.D. from MIT Media Lab in May, 2020, advised by Professor Alex 'Sandy' Pentland. I hold dual Master's degrees in Computer Science and Transportation Engineering from MIT and Bachelor's degrees in Transportation Engineering and Computational Mathematics from Beijing Jiaotong University.

I am looking for highly-motivated visiting scholars to work together on active learning problems on social networks in management applications. Please email me with your CV if you are interested in this topic.
